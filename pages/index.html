Explore Geneva's odor reports in real time.
Use the interactive map to view, submit, and track odor's within the community.

How to Report an Odor Event:

Simply click on the map to place a pin and register the odor. Location and time are automatically recorded. Add comments if you wish. Click "Show All Markers" to view all submitted reports (this may take a moment to load).

Go to Map

Your Privacy Matters

Anonymous Reporting: When you submit an odor report, any additional comments or details you provide will not be displayed publicly on the map or shared with other users.

Your comments help us better understand patterns and severity, but remain confidential. Only the location, time, intensity, and duration levels are shown to the community.
